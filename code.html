ABOUT

In 2006, Georgia Anne Muldrow released the debut EP, Worthnothings, on Stones Throw Records. Her first album, Olesi: Fragments of an Earth, was released on the label in that year. She released Seeds, an album entirely produced by Madlib, on SomeOthaShip Connect in 2012.
