About

I started coding in C++ while graduating in Materials Science, a subject involving Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics applied to the development of new materials. I have been working in sales of electronics systems for the past four years and in the process learnt how to design, assemble and program a variety of electronics systems, working as a freelance developer. Along the way I picked up more languages and frameworks to suit different needs, such as

  • Creative coding frameworks (Openframework , Processing, LXStudio.. some code here)
  • OpenGL and WebGL
  • Python
  • Mql4
  • Scio Molecular Spectroscopy Dev Kit
  • PLC (programmable logic controller) and prototype building languages (Arduino, Splat controls, Allen Bradley)
  • Html, CSS, and JavaScript (Vanilla and JQuery, currently studying ReactJS and other Front-End frameworks)


I love solving problems in efficient, neat and creative ways.

I live in Melbourne, Australia.
